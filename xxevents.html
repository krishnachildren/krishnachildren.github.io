Concepts and Principles of Gita

  • A 12 Session Course for Children of Standard 5th to 12th

    09 Jan 2022 to 27 Mar 2022 Every Sunday Afternoon for 90 minutes ISKCON Kanpur
    • Would you like to see your child transforming into a thoughtful personality with a mature conscience?
    • Would you like to see your child being able to take decisions in his/her life using the timeless wisdom of Bhagavad Gita and lessons from the lives of great personalities?
    • Would you like to see your child remain happy and fearless even in the face of greatest challenges in life?
    • Would you like to prepare your child for the test called life?

    If yes, this course is for your child.

    Course Curriculum

    Concepts

    • Nature – Prakriti: What is the fundamental nature of this world? Electrons, protons, quarks or even beyond.
    • Living being – Jiva
    • Why is life so special? Stones do not inquire about the nature of the universe, but humans set up universities to learn about it.
    • The Supreme Lord – Isvara
    • What is the ultimate principle sustaining the universe? Is that accessible to us?
    • Time – Kala
    • From ancient sundials to Swiss watches, great minds, including Newton and Einstein, have been studying what is time. From another perspective, people talk of time management and timelines. But what is time?
    • Actions – Karma
    • Laziness is death, action is life. But not every action achieves reward. What is the art of right action that leads one to perfection?
